About

Jane is a versatile and committed theatre actress with eight years of professional experience. Specializing in classical plays and contemporary drama, she has performed at regional theatres, off-Broadway productions, and celebrated festivals across the country.

Known for her depth of character work and collaborative spirit, Jane brings intelligence and nuance to every role. She is passionate about storytelling that challenges audiences and creates meaningful theatrical experiences.

Jane holds a BFA in Acting and has trained extensively in voice, movement, and Meisner technique. When not performing, she teaches acting and mentors emerging artists.
